## The Data

#####Subject
Subject who performed the activity for each window sample. Range is from 1 to 30. 


#####Activity
Type of Activity performed in the test. Range is from:

* WALKING
* WALKING_UPSTAIRS
* WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
* SITTING
* STANDING
* LAYING
